Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the reset link (check spam folder)
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Login with your new credentials

Note: The reset link expires in 1 hour for security.

How can I update my profile information? +

To update your profile:

  • Login to your account
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right
  • Select "Edit Profile"
  • Update your information
  • Click "Save Changes"

Some information (like email) may require verification before changes take effect.

What are the membership fees? +

Arewa Digital Hub offers different membership levels:

  • Basic (Free): Access to community forums and basic courses
  • Premium (‚Ç¶5,000/month): All courses, mentorship, and job board access
  • Enterprise (Custom pricing): For organizations and teams

All prices are in Nigerian Naira. We offer discounts for students and annual subscriptions.

How do I report inappropriate content or behavior? +

We take community safety seriously. To report issues:

  1. Click the "Report" button next to the content
  2. Select the reason for reporting
  3. Provide additional details if needed
  4. Submit the report

For urgent matters, email safety@arewahub.com or use the emergency contact number.

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

Can I cancel my subscription? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Subscriptions
  • Click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Follow the prompts to confirm

Important:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing cycle
  • No refunds for partial months
  • You'll retain access until the end of your paid period
How do I join community events? +

Participating in community events is easy:

  1. Visit the Events section in your dashboard
  2. Browse upcoming events (virtual and physical)
  3. Click "Register" for events you want to attend
  4. You'll receive confirmation and event details

Some events may have limited seating or require approval.

Physical events in Northern Nigeria: We regularly host events in Kano, Kaduna, Abuja, and other northern states.
